## Resolving 404 errors on category pages

If your category pages are returning a 404 error, open Platform to resolve the issue:

1. Click **Catalog** in the main menu.
1. In the next blade, select the catalog associated with your store.
1. In the next blade, click on the three dots to the left of any category name and select **Manage** from the dropdown menu.
1. In the next blade, click on the **SEO** widget.
1. In the next blade, select the SEO entry.

    ![SEO error](media/404-error.png)

1. In the next blade, delete the associated store in the **Store** field.
1. Click **OK** to save the changes. 

    ![Delete store](media/delete-store.png)

1. Click **Save** in the previous blade.

Once these steps are completed, your category pages should load correctly without returning a 404 error.
