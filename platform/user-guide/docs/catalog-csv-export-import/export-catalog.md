# Export Catalog

To export your catalog in a CSV format:

1. Open **Catalog** in the main menu.
1. In the next blade, select your catalog.
1. In the next blade, check the required categories or products.
1. Click **Export** in the toolbar.
1. In the next blade, click **Virto Commerce CSV export**. 

    !!! note
        If you select **Generic Export** type, your catalog will be exported via the **Export** module. 

        ![Readmore](media/readmore.png){: width="25"} [Exporting via Export module](../generic-export/overview.md)

1. In the next blade, select:
    1. CSV column delimiter.
    1. Fulfillment center for exported inventory.
    1. Price list for exported prices.

1. Click **Start export**.
1. After exporting is finished, download the generated file.

![Catalog export](media/catalog-export.gif)

Your catalog has been downloaded.
