# Import Catalog

To import your catalog in a CSV format:

1. Open **Catalog** in the main menu.
1. In the next blade, select your catalog.
1. In the next blade, check the required categories.
1. Click **Import** in the toolbar.
1. In the next blade, click **VirtoCommerce CSV import**.
1. In the next blade: 
    1. Select a delimiter from the dropdown list.
    1. Upload your CSV file.
    1. Click **Map columns**.
1. In the next blade, verify that the columns are properly mapped, then click **OK**.
1. In the **Import catalog from CSV** blade, click **Start import**.

![CSV upload](media/catalog-import.gif)

Your catalog has been successfully imported.
