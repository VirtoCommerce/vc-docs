# Overview  

Virto Commerce offers flexible ways to purchase products tailored to various customer needs:  

1. **Quick purchase from the product list**: Users add products to their carts directly from the product list.   

    ![Quick purchase](media/quick-product-purchase.png)

    ![Readmore](media/readmore.png){: width="25"} [Products management](managing-products.md)

1. **Choose from product variations**: Users select the specific variation of a product that best fits their requirements, such as size, color, or style. 

    ![Product variations](media/product-variations.png)

    ![Readmore](media/readmore.png){: width="25"}  [Product variations management](managing-product-variations.md)

1. **Customize products to your needs**: Users adapt products to their preferences by configuring available options, such as selecting decorations for a cake or features for a bike. 

    ![Product configurations](media/customize-button.png)

    ![Readmore](media/readmore.png){: width="25"} [Product configurations management](managing-product-configurations.md)
