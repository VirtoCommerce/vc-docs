# AI Assistant

The AI Assistant is available through the AI Assistant button in the toolbar on several operational blades in the Vendor Portal to help automate routine tasks and simplify data management.

The AI Assistant can perform different actions based on user prompts. At present, the following capabilities are available:

* **Search and analysis**: Locate orders, products, or offers using natural language queries, and perform simple analytical requests such as calculating totals or summarizing data for a specific time period.

* **Product management**: Generate product descriptions, translate descriptions and localized product names, and automatically populate product properties.

* **Offer management**: Create offers for products with specified prices and inventory levels, distribute stock across multiple warehouses when applicable, and support date-based offer availability. The assistant can also update existing offers by modifying prices or inventory values.
